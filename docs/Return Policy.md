# Blue Penguin – Refund & Return Policy

At Blue Penguin, each piece is thoughtfully handcrafted. Because many of our products are made-to-order or produced in small batches, we maintain a careful and transparent return policy.

---

## 1. Returns Eligibility

We accept returns only under the following conditions:

- The item was received damaged or defective.
- The wrong item was delivered.
- A manufacturing defect is identified within 48 hours of delivery.

To request a return, customers must contact us within 48 hours of receiving the product and provide:

- Order details
- Clear photos or videos showing the issue
- Unboxing proof (if available)

Items must be unused, unworn, and returned in original packaging.

---

## 2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

- Custom-made or personalized jewelry
- Size-adjusted or specially modified pieces
- Items purchased during limited offers or clearance sales
- Minor variations in color, texture, or finish due to the handmade nature

Please note: Slight variations are natural in handcrafted products and are not considered defects.

---

## 3. Refund Policy

If a return is approved:

- Refunds will be processed to the original payment method.
- Processing time: 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
- Shipping charges (if applicable) are non-refundable.

If a replacement is preferred and available, we will ship a replacement at no additional product cost.

---

## 4. Return Shipping

- If the return is due to our error (damaged or incorrect item), we will cover return shipping.
- In all other approved cases, return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.

---

## 5. Order Cancellations

- Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placing the order.
- Custom or personalized orders cannot be cancelled once production has begun.

---

## 6. Contact Information

To request a return or report an issue, please contact us via:

- Instagram DM
- WhatsApp
- Email: blue.penguin.india@gmail.com

Please include your order number and clear details of the issue.
