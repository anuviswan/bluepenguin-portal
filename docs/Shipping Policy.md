# Blue Penguin – Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

At Blue Penguin, every piece is thoughtfully handcrafted and carefully packed. Because many of our items are made-to-order or produced in limited batches, shipping timelines may vary slightly.

---

## 1. Order Processing Time

- Ready-to-ship items: Dispatched within 2–4 business days.
- Made-to-order items: Dispatched within 5–10 business days.
- Custom or personalized pieces: Processing time will be communicated at the time of order.

Orders are processed on business days only (excluding Sundays and public holidays).

You will receive confirmation once your order has been shipped.

---

## 2. Shipping Timeline

Estimated delivery times after dispatch:

- Within Kerala: 2–4 business days
- Rest of India: 3–7 business days

Delivery timelines may vary depending on courier services and location accessibility.

---

## 3. Shipping Charges

- Shipping charges (if applicable) will be communicated at the time of order.
- Free shipping may be offered during special promotions or above certain order values.

---

## 4. Order Tracking

Once shipped, tracking details will be shared via:

- WhatsApp
- Instagram DM
- Email (if provided)

Customers are responsible for ensuring accurate delivery details at the time of placing the order.

---

## 5. Delays & External Factors

While we strive to deliver on time, delays may occur due to:

- Courier service disruptions
- Weather conditions
- Public holidays
- Incomplete or incorrect address details

Blue Penguin is not liable for delays caused by external courier partners, but we will always assist in tracking and resolving delivery issues.

---

## 6. Delivery Issues

If:

- The package appears damaged at the time of delivery, please record an unboxing video.
- The package is marked as delivered but not received, contact us within 24 hours.

We will coordinate with the courier partner to investigate and resolve the issue.

---

## 7. International Shipping

Currently, we ship only within India.  
For international orders, please contact us directly to discuss availability and shipping costs.
